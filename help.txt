```
NeonBot Guide:

+pay: 
Transer Neons to someone.
Add an "N" to send Neons or an "n" to send Neyonnies.
(+pay @person 5N)
(+pay @person 500n)

+give: 
Give someone an item that you own.
(+give @person 10 chicken)

+balance: 
Check how much of an item you have.
(+balance)
(+balance all)
(+balance rice)
(+balance money)

+add:
Add something to your inventory.
(+add 5 crabs)
```
